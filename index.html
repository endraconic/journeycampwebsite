We offer a program of friendship and mentorship that has empowered girls and non-binary youth for over 30 years. Our large staff of teen leaders, junior staff, college age, and adults provide individualized support as they teach how to care for yourself and care for each other.

Our Program

Each day we build stick houses on the Pocumtuck Ridge, play games, sing, tell stories, and learn how to talk things out. Workshop choices include art, dance hikes, writing, and story-building. From the start we make sure everyone feels included so there is a solid feeling of belonging and social safety.
